Lola's favorite movies

My top of 2025:

This year I didn't watch too many movies, but I can recommend the best ones for you to entertain yourself.

Bring Her Back

It's an Australian horror movie. It's about two brothers who discover a dark ritual at the home of their new adoptive mother and is a hit of recent horror cinema.

Jurassic World: Rebirth

It's a science fiction adventure film. It's about a new generation of dinosaurs that have evolved and are now living in a new world.

Five Nights at Freddy's 2

It's a horror movie based on the popular video game series. It's about a security guard who must survive five nights in a haunted pizzeria filled with animatronic characters that come to life at night.
